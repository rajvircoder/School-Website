Florence Nightingale visit
Year 2 Florence Nightingale visit.

"Thursday 25th May year 2 was visited by Florence Nightingale. Using a selection of Victorian artefacts, Miss Nightingale told the children about her early life, her dreams of becoming a nurse and then take them back in time to experience life as it was for her in Scutari. The children, dressed up as nurses, soldiers, and doctors, whilst learning about the health hazards of Victorian life.
